Our Story

Where Tradition Meets
Modern Elegance

Mastalina Fashion Store was born from a love for the rich craft traditions of South Asian ethnic wear. We bring the finest South Asian occasion wear, bridal collections, and festive suits to the heart of Bangalore.

Every piece in our collection is handpicked — from the delicate embroidery of georgette party suits to the opulent zari work of bridal shararas. We believe every woman deserves to feel extraordinary at every celebration.

Located in Frazer Town, Bangalore, we cater to women who dress with intention — for weddings, festivals, Eid, family functions, and everyday moments that deserve beauty.
